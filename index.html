About Us

McDowell Surveyors is an independent Marine and Cargo Surveying Firm that provides quality services to the Maritime Industry.  
Established in Dominican Republic in 1969, we have served the local and international customers in ports in the Dominican Republic, Haiti, Jamaica and Puerto Rico through strategic alliances in the Caribbean.

Our many years of experience in the maritime field makes us confident to provide efficient and rapid response to your needs in any situation.
In 2017, McDowell Surveyors incorporated regional offices in Florida, to provide full services and faster response to the US Market as well as ports all around the Caribbean.

OUR VISION

To be the Firm of choice for Marine Surveying, Cargo Inspection and Maritime Consultancy Services in the Caribbean Region.

OUR MISSION

To provide the best services and contribute towards reducing risks and liability exposure through accurate and consistent expertise.
